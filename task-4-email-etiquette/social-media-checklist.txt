Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful profile.
2. Verify information before sharing it online.
3. Use privacy settings to control your personal data.
4. Be polite and respectful in online interactions.
5. Think before posting any content.
6. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Do not spread fake news or unverified content.
3. Do not post offensive or inappropriate material.
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying or harassment.
5. Do not misuse or share others’ data without permission.
6. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments.
