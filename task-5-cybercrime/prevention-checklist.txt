Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or services.
4. Check payment requests carefully before approving them.
5. Use official apps and avoid third-party or unknown applications.
6. Enable SMS and app notifications for all transactions.
7. Regularly monitor your bank account for unusual activity.
8. Report any cybercrime immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
